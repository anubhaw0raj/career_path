Chartered Accountancy

Chartered Accountancy is a professional course in accounting that prepares individuals to become certified accountants. CAs are responsible for financial reporting, auditing, taxation, and financial management.

Pros

  • High demand in various industries
  • Good earning potential
  • Opportunities for self-employment

Cons

  • Challenging examinations and study process
  • Work can be repetitive and deadline-driven
  • Continuous updates required on financial laws and regulations
